About Jain Mool Sangh

Rooted in the sacred traditions of the Digambar Jain community and inspired by Mool Sangh, our organization stands as a beacon of hope and positive transformation in today's world.

"Ahimsa paramo dharma" - Non-violence is the supreme virtue
